Roulette Directional Distance Predictor

Spins are assumed to alternate direction (CW, CCW, CW, CCW, ...). For each upcoming spin we:
• Look at the last 3 spins in that same direction,
• Use a quadratic extrapolation of their wheel positions to predict the next pocket,
• Apply a loss mode that flips to the opposite side of the wheel after blocks of 4 losses,
• Run a Martingale on stake per number, resetting to base after 11 consecutive losses.

This is the initial stake per number for both Live and Simulator. Martingale doubles it on each losing bet, and after 11 consecutive losing bets it resets to this base amount.
