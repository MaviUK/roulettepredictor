Roulette Fixed-Zone (17 Numbers) Predictor

This version does not use any maths model. It scans the wheel and selects a fixed centre number and its 8 neighbours each side (17 numbers total) that produces the smallest worst “miss run” on your spin history. Target: find a zone that never goes 11 spins without a hit (if possible from your data).
Martingale: doubles stake per number after each losing bet; resets after 11 consecutive losses.

Base = starting stake per number for Live, Simulator, and CSV runs. Martingale doubles this after each losing bet, and resets after 11 consecutive losses.

Current model: Fixed 17-number zone finder (centre + 8 neighbours). Target: no 11-spin miss run (if possible).
