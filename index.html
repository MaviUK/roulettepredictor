Roulette Directional Distance Predictor

Spins are assumed to alternate direction (CW, CCW, CW, CCW, ...). For each spin it records the wheel distance travelled in that spin's direction. To predict the next spin, it:
• Finds the last up to 5 distances in the same direction as the upcoming spin,
• Uses a recency-weighted, trimmed average blended with the mode (most common distance),
• If the current losing streak reaches 4+, it switches the prediction to the opposite side of the wheel (and flips back after another 4 losses),
• Moves that many pockets from the last result in the upcoming direction,
• Tells you to cover that centre and its 8 neighbours each side (17 numbers).
